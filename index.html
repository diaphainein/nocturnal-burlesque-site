Explore the Darkness

Welcome to The Nocturnal Tease Burlesque Festival

A weekend long celebration of all things dark and spectacular

April 25th & 26th, 2020 in Dallas, Texas


Come and experience a diverse cast, amazing vendors and events, as well as carefully curated classes to help you bring your darkest dreams to life.


As a sound and an aesthetic, goth was always on the fringe. Itâ€™s earliest recognizable form was during the early days of glam rock - the baroque fantasy fetishizing Victorian garb morphing into sci-fi delight and sequins. Queer icons like Marc Bolan and David Bowie would shape that early image of goth-glam, these influences are still felt in modern iterations. Goth would be reborn in the era of post-punk by icons like The Cure, Siouxsie and the Banshees, Bauhaus, Depeche Mode, Joy Division and countless other groups.


Nocturnal Burlesque Festival will focus on honoring those roots as well as celebrating how each of our performers carry these sounds and visions into the future. Our mission as a festival is to uplift the voices of queer performers and performers of color - To create a space where all are safe and united in celebrating the art and music we all love.

Performer Info

As a performer, you will have the opportunity to showcase your talents at one of two incredible venues here in Dallas.

We will offer a stipend for our performers, full access to our schedule of classes on Saturday, as well as a walking ghost tour of Deep Ellum (a historic art neighborhood south of downtown Dallas) after a celebratory brunch (location TBD).

In addition to these perks you will receive professional photo of you number by Mark Kaplan and video of your numbers by Ben Britt.
