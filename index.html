Explore the Darkness

Welcome to The Nocturnal Tease Burlesque Festival

A weekend long celebration of all things dark and spectacular

November 13th & 14th, 2020 in Dallas, Texas


Come and experience a diverse cast, amazing vendors and events, as well as carefully curated classes to help you bring your darkest dreams to life.


As a sound and an aesthetic, goth was always on the fringe. Its earliest recognizable form was during the early days of glam rock - the baroque fantasy fetishizing Victorian garb morphing into sci-fi delight and sequins. Queer icons like Marc Bolan and David Bowie would shape that early image of goth-glam, these influences are still felt in modern iterations. Goth would be reborn in the era of post-punk by icons like The Cure, Siouxsie and the Banshees, Bauhaus, Depeche Mode, Joy Division and countless other groups.


Nocturnal Burlesque Festival will focus on honoring those roots, as well as celebrating how each of our performers carry these sounds and visions into the future. Our mission as a festival is to uplift the voices of queer performers and performers of color - to create a space where all are safe and united in celebrating the art and music we all love.

About Us

The Nocturnal Burlesque Festival Staff

Learn more about the people that make the Nocturnal Burlesque Festival a reality!

Vivienne Vermuth

Producer

Pronouns - She/Her

Vivienne is the executive Producer of the monthly Vixen Lounge Burlesque, the weekly Tuesday Tease at Sue Ellens, and the yearly Nearly Naked Nutcracker! As a solo artist, she has won Best Burlesque in Denton 2018, Denton Arts Magazine, Best Costume 2018 Oklahoma City Burlesque Fest, Pineapple Sparkle Award (to represent community, graciousness and warmth towards others) at 2018 Texas Queerlesque Festival. Vivienne is a lifetime member of the Robert Smith fan club, and is incredibly excited about bringing this fest to life.

Chordella Smash

Co-Producer and The Red Right Hand

Chordella Smash is a 6 year veteran of DFW Queerlesque. As a producer- Chordella’s core belief is that art should be accessible, inclusive, and representative as hell. Over the course of her time in burlesque she has led workshops on self acceptance and body positivity, produced shows, acted as a stage manager, costumer, photographer, and has shaken ass all over the state.

The Tasselled Squirrel

Co-Producer and Mischief Manager

A long time lover of the occult, gothic, and spooky aesthetic, Tasseled Squirrel has been in burlesque almost 10 years. Staring with her 1st production in Los Angeles, Dead Girl Burlesque, she has produced and performed in a variety of capacities over her career. She is honored to be helping produce the inaugural Nocturnal Tease Festival.
